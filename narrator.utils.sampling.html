moving-quantiles

fn

[]
[{:keys [quantiles window sample-size], :or {quantiles [0.5 0.9 0.95 0.99 0.999]}, :as options}]

Emits the statistical distribution of messages, weighted towards those in the last window milliseconds.

moving-sample

fn

[]
[{:keys [window sample-size], :or {sample-size 1024, window (t/hours 1)}}]

Emits a sampling of messages, weighted toward those in the last window milliseconds.

quantiles

fn

[]
[{:keys [quantiles clear-on-reset? sample-size], :or {quantiles [0.5 0.9 0.95 0.99 0.999]}, :as options}]

Emits the statistical distribution of messages. If clear-on-reset? is true, this only represents the distribution of messages in the last period, otherwise it represents the distribution over the entire lifetime of the stream.

sample

fn

[]
[{:keys [clear-on-reset? sample-size], :or {clear-on-reset? true, sample-size 1024}}]

Emits a uniform sampling of messages. If clear-on-reset? is true, the sample only represents messages in the last period, otherwise the sample is over the entire lifetime of the stream.
